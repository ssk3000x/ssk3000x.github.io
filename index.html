Welcome to Top Binz

Top Binz is a mobile app that enables soccer players to upload videos of their shooting sessions and receive detailed accuracy and precision metrics, along with a visual heatmap of shot placement. These insights help players analyze performance, identify patterns, and refine their shooting technique over time.

Download For IOS

Powerful Performance Metrics

Heatmap Analysis 🥅

Visualize exactly where your shots are landing on the goal, helping you identify your strengths and weaknesses at a glance.

Accuracy Score 🎯

Shows how close your shots are to the goal corners displaying your shot-on-goal performance.

Precision Metrics 📏

Our custom clustering algorithm reveals whether you can reliably hit the same spot consistently.

Your Privacy Matters

At Top Binz, your privacy is paramount. We only collect the coordinates of the goalpost and soccer ball from your videos. We want to be clear: we do not store any information from selected videos themselves or any of their frames. Your data is used solely to provide performance metrics and insights directly to you. For more information, please see our full Privacy Policy.
