Welcome to Top Binz

Top Binz is a mobile app that enables soccer players to upload videos of their shooting sessions and receive detailed accuracy and precision metrics, along with a visual heatmap of shot placement. These insights help players analyze performance, identify patterns, and refine their shooting technique over time.

Your Privacy Matters

At Top Binz, your privacy is paramount. We only collect the coordinates of the goalpost and soccer ball from your videos and never store the videos themselves. Your data is used solely to provide performance metrics directly to you. For full details, please read our complete Privacy Policy.
